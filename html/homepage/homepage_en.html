How does this work?

MULibrary Search provides an easy way to search for:

  • books
  • eBooks
  • course & textbook reserves
  • streaming media
  • dvds
  • government documents
  • select journal and newspaper articles
  • a specific journal

Sign-in to your MyLibrary account in order to:

  • view full search results
  • renew books and videos
  • request materials from other libraries
  • export citations to RefWorks
  • create favorites lists
